## Solution Overview
The Vocal and Behavioral Candidate Evaluation System (VBCUA) is an advanced educational evaluation engine that processes candidate audio to provide comprehensive, multi-modal feedback. 

Unlike traditional tools that only look at text, VBCUA evaluates **how** a candidate speaks (acoustic confidence via RMS and ZCR) and **what** they say (semantic similarity via Sentence-BERT). It supplements quantitative data with qualitative insights from the Gemini Pro API. The entire system is packaged into a sleek, visually stunning Streamlit dashboard that allows candidates to track their progress over time and download PDF reports.
